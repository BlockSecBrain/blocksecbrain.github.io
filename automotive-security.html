Automotive Security Assessment

Modern vehicles are increasingly connected, making them potential targets for cyberattacks. At BlockSecBrain, our automotive security assessments protect vehicles, ECUs, infotainment systems, and connected services from security risks. We perform penetration testing, vulnerability analysis, and secure design reviews to ensure safe and resilient automotive systems.

ECU & Embedded Systems Testing

Analyse the security of Electronic Control Units, embedded controllers, and firmware to detect vulnerabilities and hardcoded secrets.

🔌

CAN Bus & Network Analysis

Inspect in-vehicle communication networks — CAN, LIN, and Ethernet protocols — for injection attacks, replay attacks, and weak authentication.

📡

Connected Services & Telematics

Assess telematics, infotainment, mobile apps, and cloud services integrated with the vehicle for secure communication and data protection.

🔐

Access Control & Authorisation

Review authentication mechanisms, user roles, and secure key management to prevent unauthorised access to vehicle systems.

🛠️

Vulnerability Exploitation & Penetration

Perform controlled penetration testing to identify exploitable vulnerabilities in ECUs, networks, and connected interfaces.

📊

Compliance & Risk Assessment

Provide actionable recommendations and risk mitigation strategies to comply with ISO/SAE 21434 and UNECE WP.29 automotive security standards.
